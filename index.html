Features

Everything in one place.

The library. The developmental map. The calendar. The caregiver plan. All of it.

Development dashboard

Your child's progress across every domain, mapped to validated frameworks. Full view or Think / Relate / Move. See where they're strong. See where they need work. See the rate of movement over time.

Lesson library

Every quality enrichment activity from Khan Academy, Teachers Pay Teachers, PBS Kids, museum curricula, library programs — in one place. Every activity tagged by age and developmental domain. Direct link to the provider. Five Checks scored.

Five Checks quality scoring

Every lesson is scored across five dimensions: Rigor, Inclusivity, Perspective, Empathy, Critical Thinking. AI scores lessons on ingest. Parents refine scores and add context — turning a 2.5 into a 5.

Developmental calendar

Plan by the day, month, quarter, or year. Recurring classes — swim lessons, gymnastics — sit alongside one-time events and open activity slots. Your child's development drives what gets scheduled.

Caregiver mode — no login, no decisions

Share a 6-character code. Grandma opens the link. She sees today's plan — already made. What to do. What to say to start. What success looks like. What to do if it goes sideways. She executes. She doesn't decide. She feels capable. You see what happened.
